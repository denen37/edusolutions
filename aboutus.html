About Us

Who are we

JITsolutions

JITSolutions is a premier IT Training, Certification, Networking, and Software Development company in Nigeria. As an authorized partner of global IT leaders such as Cisco, Oracle, Pearson VUE, and Prometric, we deliver top-tier training, certification exams, and bespoke web application development services. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations through cutting-edge IT solutions. Our team comprises seasoned software engineers and project managers with extensive industry experience, committed to delivering user-friendly solutions that adhere to the highest quality standards.

Features

Our key features

Student Profile & Record Management

Easily manage comprehensive student data including biodata, academic history, medical records, class assignments, and session details.

Result Management

Easily manage continuous assessments (CA), and final exam scores. Edusolutions enables schools to customize grading systems, generate report cards with graphical analysis.

Administration Management

Digitize the entire enrollment process—from online applications and document uploads to automated approval notifications.

Staff Management

Manage all staff profiles, from recruitment to employment details, roles, class assignments, attendance, and staff member’s responsibilities.

Calendar & Event Management

Keep everyone informed with a smart calendar that tracks exams, PTA meetings, holidays, view upcoming activities and school-wide events.

e-Exam / CBT (Computer-Based Test)

Conduct secure online assessments. With built-in question banks, and auto-grading which allows schools to manage exams while ensuring fairness and integrity.

E-Library

Offer students and staff access to a digital library filled with educational resources such as PDFs, videos, audio materials, and interactive lessons. The e-Library promotes independent learning.

Bulk Messaging

Send instant messages to parents, teachers, and students via SMS or email. Notifications can include updates on attendance, results, fees, and events.

Enquiry Management

Receive and respond to inquiries from prospective students and parents with ease. The system automatically stores communication records and sends timely responses.

Alumni Record Management

Stay connected with your graduates by maintaining detailed alumni profiles. Build a strong alumni network for events, mentorship, donations, or school development initiatives.

Tuition Record Management

Digitize the entire enrollment process—from online applications and document uploads to automated approval notifications.

Why EduSolutions

It includes a standard website and unique domain name to promote your school to a vast local and global audience. Developed by a team of experienced project managers, software engineers, and designers, Edusolutions is the result of 15 years of dedicated research and development.
